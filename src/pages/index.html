This tool helps you to manage and filter your rom collection by removing unwanted, bad, and unplayable games.
It assumes you have a working, nonmerged romset corresponding to the MAME/FBA version you're using.
If you have no idea what a "romset" is, what "nonmerged" means, or why the MAME version is important, check out the help section first.
